simpler.pw

How to simply generate a new password:

  1. Choose a public username, e.g. "spock" (it can be any word you like)
  2. Choose a secret password, e.g. "green socks" (keep this secret!)
  3. Enter your public username and secret password at simpler.pw/password
  4. Enter a short memorable word, e.g. "news", to generate a secure password

Your secret password is not stored anywhere; it is only used to generate secure passwords from your memorable words.
